Animal Lick Salt

We have the best natural lick salt is unprocessed and without any chemical additive or alteration , purely organic and inexpensive as cattle nutrient.  They provide cattle and animals the abundance of required minerals for healthy growth and production. Pink Himalayan Lick Salt is the perfect salt nutrition and mineral supplement for horses, cattles , and other livestock. With all the vital nutrients required by the animals at all life stages, Lick Salt is an essential part of daily feed.

Lick Salt is especially important in rough weather and environment with poor possibility of the much needed nutrients, as well as elements such as phosphorus, sodium, calcium, iron, zinc and other trace elements required for bones , muscles and other formation in animals. Lactating cows, for instance, are particularly accessible to salt deficiency and every day, they need lick salt to make milk which contains a good quantity of sodium and chloride.
