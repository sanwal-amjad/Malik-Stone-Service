Difference b/w Himalayan Pink Salt & Celtic Sea Salt :

  • Himalayan Pink Salt is of Pink color with unique hues of orange and celtic sea salt is of light grey color with subtle hues of purple color.
  • Himalayan salt contains 84 trace minerals whereas celtic sea salt contains just 34 trace minerals in its purest form, some are added in purifying process.
  • Himalayan salt contains 0.026% residual moisture whereas celtic sea salt contains 13% of moisture.
  • Himalayan salt has 98% of soium chloride and celtic sea salt contains 33% sodium ,50.9% chloride.
  • Himalayan salt is used in its purest form so the minerals remain intact while Celtics sea salt lost its minerals while purification purpose.
  • Himalayan salt contains natural Iodine while artificial iodine is added in sea salt.
